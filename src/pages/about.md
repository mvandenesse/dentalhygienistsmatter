# About

**DentalHygienistsMatter.com** exists to share clear, sourced information about proposed dental workforce legislation and its potential impact on **patient safety**, **standards of care**, and **informed consent**.

## Principles
- Be accurate and cite sources.
- Use plain English.
- Focus on policy and patient impact.
